\subsection{Detailed Description}
Lateral controller for controlling the lateral position of the vehicle to stay on the desired lane. Therefore a suitable steering angle is chosen based on the current state of the vehicle. 

Longitudinal controller for controlling the longitudinal position of the vehicle. Two driving scenarios can be used, namely follow-\/up mode or free driving. Therefore a suitable activation level of the gas pedal is chosen based on the current state of the vehicle.
